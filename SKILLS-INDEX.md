## Frameworks Reference

### Shreyas Doshi
- **LNO Framework**: Leverage (10-100x return), Neutral (1x), Overhead (<1x)
- **Pre-Mortem**: Imagine failure, identify causes, mitigate proactively
- **Opportunity Cost > ROI**: "Is this the BEST use of my time?" not just "good"
- **Three Levels of Product Work**: Impact, Execution, Optics
- **High Agency**: Ownership mindset + creative execution

### Lenny Rachitsky
- **Magic Loop**: Framework for rapid career growth
- **ARMOR**: Resilience framework for handling burnout
- **AI-First PM**: PRD + Markdown system for keeping AI agents aligned

### Aakash Gupta
- **7 Layers of PLG**: Activation, Retention, Monetization, Expansion, + 3 more
- **Growth Team Philosophy**: Metric-driven vs. artifact-driven teams
- **OKR Sizing**: Commit to slightly stretch OKRs

### Nate B Jones
- **AI-First Product Strategy**: LLM breakthroughs into real-world impact
- **Volume > Perfection**: Rapid iteration; hesitation is the real risk
- **Prompt Engineering for PMs**: Double productivity through AI workflows

### Teresa Torres / Marty Cagan
- **Opportunity Solution Tree**: Map opportunities to solutions
- **Continuous Discovery**: Weekly customer touchpoints
- **Empowered Teams**: Solve problems, not just ship features

### Gibson Biddle
- **DHM Framework**: Delight, Hard-to-copy, Margin-enhancing
